## Step 8: Real Estate Layer — Macro View

**Chatbox prompt (default):**
宏觀判斷（2025–2030）

Kumamoto is in "supply shock" phase:
- **Wave 1 (now):** Construction workforce + JASM Fab 1 early employees
- **Wave 2 (~2027):** Fab 2 brings higher-income engineers & R&D (higher housing quality demand)
- **Gap conclusion:** Local construction capacity limited; supply catches up ~2028-2029
- Focus: medium-long term stable growth, not short-term speculation

### Current Investment Areas

**A. Kikuyo:** Corporate RC condos + 3LDK/4LDK detached houses
- Trend: RC Mansion (near new station/park) + expatriate family detached houses
- Target: single engineers, long-term & short-mid term assignees

**B. Ozu:** Transit hub employee accommodation
- Trend: Airport + rail link makes it supply chain company employee housing choice
- Product: 4LDK detached houses as flexible company dormitories

**C. Koshi:** Family-type products
- Trend: R&D engineer families (TEL etc.), prefer quiet, school district, amenities
- Product: 3LDK condos or mid-high spec detached houses

### Watch Areas

**Hikarinomori (光之森):**
- Opportunity: High-spec detached rental or renovation to "expatriate standard"
- Trend: Large mall drives amenities, but "internationalized services" still developing

**Kumamoto Station (熊本站):**
- Opportunity: RC Mansion condos + hotels (business travelers, regional managers)
- Trend: 30-min Shinkansen to Hakata; "Kyushu-level business support center"

### Why MoreHarvest

- Avoid danger zones (narrow roads, flood areas)
- Market-responsive products
- 20 years investment development experience
- RC / BTR / Renovation flexibility

## Product Planning Context

### Mission
Transform top-tier real estate fund decision-making into scalable, verifiable, long-term intelligent service.

### Product Positioning
AI-native Investment Decision & Execution OS for real estate. Not a mapping tool, not a data platform — an intelligent decision service.

### Core Value
**Private Data** (from every step and every cycle) + **Entry Point** (every property we build/manage/sell) = MoreHarvest decision system

### Target Customers
1. **Real Estate Brokers** — shorten transaction cycles, "expert-level evidence chains" on-site
2. **Fund Managers / IC** — auditable, reproducible, verifiable investment decisions

### Four Key Characteristics
1. **Expert-Grade** — Go/No-Go with reasons, unit mix, construction costs, IRR, exit scenarios
2. **Personalized** — adjust by user role, risk preference, deal size; "pushes you to act"
3. **Democratized** — scarce expert time → scalable delivery at low cost
4. **Continuous** — full lifecycle Pre-purchase → Holding → Exit; prediction vs. actual tracking

### Moats
- **Data Resource:** Public-private data fusion; industry-real estate correlation
- **Expertise:** Traceable assumptions, reproducible decisions, stress-testable, post-verifiable
- **Network Effects:** More transactions → more data → more accurate models → flywheel
