# Feedback round 7

## Entry / landing

The map should land on Kumamoto (not Japan overview) when the user first enters. This is a starting point fix — the current default zoom is too wide.

---

## Water resources

### Panel overlap (iPad)

On iPad 13-inch, the water resources panel is blocked by the UI, making the underlying map content invisible. The view gives the user no understanding of what is being shown. This is a critical usability failure on tablet.

### Icons

Replace generic blue dots with Suntory and Coca-Cola brand icons. This makes the data points immediately recognizable without needing to read labels, which is especially important during a live presentation.

---

## Power infrastructure

The lines connecting power infrastructure to JASM lack visual weight. Increase brightness or stroke width so they pop against the map background. The intention (showing energy supply into JASM) must be immediately legible.

---

## Airline routes

The animation rendering airline routes to other cities is noticeably slower than the previous version. This needs to be restored to the previous speed or faster.

---

## Government support

The central government representation should show a Tokyo → Kumamoto connection. Currently this relationship is not visually communicated on the map.

---

## Corporate investment

JASM should be visually dominant — larger marker, positioned as the center of the corporate ecosystem. All other corporate investments should radiate from or reference JASM. The lines to foreign cities should be removed at this step; they are a distraction and belong elsewhere.

---

## Infrastructure

### Navigation

There is no way to go back to the previous step from the infrastructure page. Back navigation must be implemented.

### Content structure

The infrastructure page needs two clear primary items:

1. Kumamoto Science Park
2. Grand Airport Concept

Clicking Kumamoto Science Park should reveal three sub-items:

- Government zone clusters
- Kikuyo long-term plan
- Ozu long-term plan

---

## Talent pipeline

The talent pipeline step needs three items:

1. Universities linking to JASM
2. Training centers and international schools (reference Miro for content)
3. Employment data (reference Miro)

---

## Future

Tapping the future step should surface all updates consolidated in the final page view.

---

## Overall — post-infrastructure

The flow becomes noticeably messy after the infrastructure page. This is the core structural issue to resolve in this round. Everything from infrastructure onward needs a clearer information hierarchy and cleaner step-to-step transitions.

---

## Priority order

1. Back navigation on infrastructure page (blocker).
2. iPad water resources panel overlap (blocker on tablet).
3. Infrastructure two-item structure with sub-items.
4. Talent pipeline three-item structure.
5. JASM as corporate center, remove foreign city lines.
6. Suntory and Coca-Cola icons.
7. Power line visual weight.
8. Government support Tokyo → Kumamoto connection.
9. Airline route animation speed.
10. Future step final page consolidation.
11. Landing map starting at Kumamoto.
